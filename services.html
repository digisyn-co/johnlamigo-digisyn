Frequently Asked Questions

Common Questions Answered

A few things clients usually want to know before we get started.

How much does a website cost? +
Most small business WordPress sites fall between $1,500 and $5,000 USD depending on scope. I'll send a fixed-scope quote within 48 hours of our discovery call — no hidden fees, no surprise invoices.
How long does a typical project take? +
A standard marketing site takes 3–5 weeks from kickoff to launch, once content is ready. WooCommerce stores and larger custom builds typically run 5–8 weeks. I'll give you a realistic timeline upfront.
Do you offer ongoing maintenance? +
Yes, but it's optional. Monthly maintenance plans are available, but I don't lock anyone into a retainer just to keep their site running. You're free to manage updates yourself or hire someone else.
Will I be able to edit the site myself? +
Absolutely. That's the whole point of building in WordPress. I hand over a clean block editor with a walkthrough video and a one-page reference, so your team can manage updates without needing a developer.
Do you handle hosting? +
I'll recommend a good host based on your traffic and budget, then set it up for you. You'll own the hosting account and credentials, so you're never locked in.
What if I need changes after launch? +
Every project includes 30 days of post-launch support for bug fixes and small tweaks. After that, additional work is billed at an hourly rate, or you can choose a monthly maintenance plan.
