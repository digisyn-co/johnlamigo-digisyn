My Story

Hi, I'm John — Nice to Meet You

I started freelancing back in 2017 while still in university. What began as a side hustle quickly turned into a full-time career, and today I run my web design practice independently while collaborating with agencies and small business owners around the world.

Over the years, I've worked with everyone from local clinics and contractors to international agencies serving the US, Canada, Australia, and beyond. The common thread? Clients who need a website that works hard for their business — not just looks pretty in a portfolio.

I specialize in WordPress because it's the honest answer for most small businesses: a CMS they can edit themselves, hosting they can choose, and a build I can hand over cleanly without locking them into a forever retainer.

My Career Journey

From Student Freelancer to Independent Pro

A quick timeline of how I got here.

2017 — 2018

Started Freelancing

Took on my first paying clients while still finishing university. Mostly WordPress builds for local Philippine businesses, then quickly expanded internationally.

2019 — 2020

Junior Wordpress Engineer at Agency

Joined an agency working on media and finance clients. Learned to ship on tight deadlines and read briefs that weren't written by designers.

2021 — 2022

Senior Wordpress Engineer

Promoted to senior. Led full builds end-to-end, ran scope discussions, wrote estimates, and mentored junior designers on the team.

2023

Back to Full-Time Freelance

Left the agency to work directly with clients. Set up my practice under DigiSyn for larger projects that need a small team.

2024 — Present

Independent & Available

Working solo or as the design+build half of a team. Mostly serving North America and Australia, with occasional projects in Europe. Currently taking on new projects this quarter.
